5.1 Head of household survey

Enumerators are asked to approach a randomly selected household within randomly selected primary sampling unit (???) and begin a survey to the head of the household. In this survey we ask information about household composition, and information about its past migration behavior. Especially, we ask about members of the household that decided to follow land and what happened to them (if they reached Europe, were imprisioned or injured along the road, or even deceased).
